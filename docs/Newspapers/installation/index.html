Installation

  1. Download weapon_newspaper and sf-paperroute from your Keymaster
  2. Add those two resources into your server.cfg:
ensure weapon_newspaper
ensure sf-paperroute
danger

Make sure that weapon_newspaper starts before sf-paperroute!

  1. Add weapon_newspaper to your inventory / framework config files.
